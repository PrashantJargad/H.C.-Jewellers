About Us

H.C. Jewellers, your destination for exquisite gemstones and custom jewellery.We specialise in both rough and polished gemstones, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, and semi-precious stones like Tanzanite and Rubellite. Our jewellery expertise ensures each piece is a unique masterpiece, tailored to your vision.

Our Story

Creating legacy

Begins in the 1960s, in the heart of Jaipur, a city steeped in cultural grandeur and gemstone heritage. From humble beginnings, our family business grew with an unwavering commitment to natural stones. What started as an expertise in rough mined emeralds soon evolved into a legacy of loose precious stones and statement jewellery pieces, each designed to celebrate the harmony between nature’s beauty and the wearer’s individuality. For three generations, we have refined our craft, ensuring that every gemstone is chosen with intent, and every design speaks of artistry. To us, jewelry isn’t just about adornment—it’s about stories, milestones, and emotions.

Our Promise

We just don’t select or create pieces or stones, we adore them, as they create someone’s legacy. They are going to be studded, passed down from one generation to another leaving the mark of their love, care and sentiment which is just not going to be worn on certain occasion but life long and beyond. We only trade in natural gemstones and provide certified gemstones or jewellery so you get your money's worth.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is simple: To deliver nothing but the finest gemstones and bespoke jewellery, with a deeply personal touch.

We believe that selecting a gemstone or commissioning a jewellery piece is a profound experience—one that should never be rushed or taken lightly. That’s why we do not sell our products online or engage in mass distribution. Each selection is a moment of trust between us and our customers, and we honor that by ensuring every gemstone meets the highest standards of quality and authenticity ​

For those who seek the finest natural gemstones and a truly bespoke jewellery experience, H.C. Jewellers is more than just a destination—it’s a legacy in the making.
